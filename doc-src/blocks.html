Much of the power and utility of Cubictemp templates lie in the use of block constructs. A block is exactly what it sounds like - a chunk of text, that may contain newlines, and that is acted on in some way by the template. Cubictemp understands two block types:

  • @!aLink("named.html", "Named blocks")!@ provide a way of "naming" a piece of text, and then performing some manipulation on it.
  • @!aLink("repeat.html", "Repeat blocks")!@ allow the insertion of sequences of data.
